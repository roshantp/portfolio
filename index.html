Our Story

At Bagaicha, we are passionate about all things plants and nature. Our company was founded by a group of gardening enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of plants with others and promote environmental sustainability.

We started out as a small plant nursery, offering a wide variety of high-quality plants for all types of gardens. Over time, we have expanded our offerings to include gardening courses, events, and programs focused on environmental conservation and sustainability.
